About Explore National Parks

This project helps users explore and discover the beauty of National Parks across the USA. You can search for parks by name, state, or activities available, and view them on an interactive map.

The goal is to provide an easy-to-use tool to inspire people to visit more national parks and learn about the diverse landscapes and activities they offer.
